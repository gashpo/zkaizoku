  • Connect your cryptocurrency wallet

  • Mint the ZKaizoku License(dNFT)

    Go to the marketplace to mint the "ZKaizoku License"(dNFT) to receive an unique avatar and the qualification of earning $ZKZ(the circulating utility tokens on this platform).(If you do not mint "ZKaizoku License", you can still play games on ZKaizoku platform and accumulate your own interaction counts on zkSync Era. However, you will NOT be able to amass $ZKZ. Consequently, this means you will not be eligible to receive our airdrops in the future.)

  • Earn $ZKZ through the interaction

    Play the games on ZKaizoku platform, and you will earn $ZKZ through the interaction of the smart contract.

  • Upgrade ZKaizoku License to gain additional multiplier effects

    Utilize your accumulated $ZKZ to upgrade ZKaizoku License to make the total amount of your $ZKZ increased through the additional multiplier effects from your ZKaizoku License upgraded.

  • Settle the zkSync Era airdrop allocation

    The result of multiplying your $ZKZ and ZKaizoku License will be divided by the total sum of $ZKZ held by all participants, determining your share on ZKaizoku platform.After zkSync Era official airdrop, we will send you your portion of TOKEN from the allocation designated for developers.
